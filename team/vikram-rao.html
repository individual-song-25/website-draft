About

Vikram Rao is the firm's dedicated ROC and MCA Specialist, and the person the entire team turns to when navigating the MCA21 V3 portal. With 5 years of focused experience in e-form filing and ROC correspondence, he has developed an encyclopaedic knowledge of MCA forms, their dependencies, timelines, and the nuances of dealing with Registrar of Companies offices across different states.

Vikram joined the firm in 2021 after completing his CS Executive programme. He is currently pursuing the CS Professional qualification from ICSI. His methodical, checklist-driven approach to filings has made him indispensable in preventing technical rejections and ensuring first-time clearance for client submissions.

Key Responsibilities

  • Filing all MCA e-forms including SPICe+, INC series, MGT, AOC, PAS, and DIR forms
  • Managing DSC (Digital Signature Certificates) procurement and renewal for clients
  • Handling ROC queries, notices, and show cause responses
  • Tracking SRN statuses and following up on pending approvals
  • Assisting in name approval, CIN allocation, and certificate of incorporation processes
  • Maintaining MCA21 V3 compliance for all active client entities

Education & Qualifications
