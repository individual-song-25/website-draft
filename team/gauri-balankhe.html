Gauri Balankhe

Founder & Principal Company Secretary

About

Gauri Balankhe is a Commerce Graduate and LLB (Gen) from Mumbai University and a Fellow Member of ICSI having over 23 yrs of experience, currently based in Bangalore. Prior to starting her own private practice in 2009, she has worked with Listed Companies such as Aarti Drugs Limited and Mangalam Drugs and Organics Limited. She had successfully handled an IPO for Mangalam Drugs and Organics Limited in the year 2005.

Her expertise and experience spreads across various areas of Company Law for Public and Private companies including Startups, Section 8 companies and LLPs. With a team of 9 members (3 Qualified, 4 Semi qualified and 2 Trainees), her firm handles all secretarial compliance including Company Incorporation, Post Incorporation Services, Due Diligence, Fund Raising, FEMA Compliances and Closures.
