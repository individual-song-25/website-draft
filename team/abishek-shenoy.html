Abishek Shenoy

About

CS Abishek Shenoy is an Associate Member of the Institute of Company Secretaries of India. He holds a Bachelor’s degree in Commerce from Mangalore University.

With close to four years of overall experience, including 1.5 years of post-qualification experience, he has developed a strong focus on corporate law, governance, and regulatory advisory. His professional expertise spans advisory, secretarial practice, and comprehensive compliance under the Companies Act, 2013. He has hands-on experience in core company law matters, including incorporation, corporate structuring, board and shareholder processes, regulatory filings, as well as dematerialisation procedures, ensuring strict adherence to statutory and governance standards.

He has a particular interest in funding round advisory, where he is actively involved in transaction structuring and the drafting of corporate documents required for the seamless execution of such transactions. Additionally, he is well-versed in FEMA-related matters, including regulatory filings and advisory pertaining to foreign investments.
